Starlight

If you catch a small star, then you will have obtained a small portion of happiness.

If you catch a large star, then you will have obtained for yourself a large fortune

If you catch both of them together, you will have obtained your dearest dream, an eternal, undying wish.

Grasping the star means forgiveness for your sins. Grasping the star is a miracle of night.

This is a story of a nearby and distant planet. It is an epic of ancient times, a drama of the far future.

There once stood a Tower. No one knows when, by whom, or for what it was built. The Tower stood under a starry sky. Standing straight, it stood as if to pierce through the moon. Standing in secret, it stood as if to refuse the starlight.

Casting its shadow onto the Tower, a star with soft spikes shimmered. A star curled up, glowing in the boiling cold. A star shining in bright darkness was waiting for someone at the Tower's top. A group of constellations spread across the sky, watching over the events to unfold..

Rin

A wolf who seeks to grasps the starlight of the tower. Having once grasped a smaller star, he gained a profound understanding of the cosmos and a glimpse of happiness, which only deepened his resolve. His eternal, undying wish drives him to seek the second, larger star, believing it holds the key to fulfilling his deepest dream.
